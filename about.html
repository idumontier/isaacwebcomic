Hey, I decided to make a short fun webcomic! The inspiration was based on my love for the outdoors and comedy. I wanted to try a few different art styles for my characters and keep the panels interesting. My artistic ability isn't the best, but I attempted to apply various techniques we learned throughout the semester. To me, making a story about polar opposite people just made sense to display various comic techniques.

I took the class as I grew up reading and collecting comic books! So naturally, spending the semester studying one of my favorite childhood hobbies only made sense.

Thanks for taking the time to click through my page!
